Website Builds

A sharp website should clarify the business, not decorate around it.

Hand-built sites in VS Code with Codex-assisted prompts, strategic copy, brand identity, and conversion-focused design — version-controlled on GitHub, deployed via GitHub Pages with a custom domain.

What This Solves

Your first digital presence needs a point of view and a next action.

A business can look established in person and still disappear online behind a generic template, unclear service list, outdated profile, or social page that asks the visitor to assemble the story themselves.

The build starts with positioning: who the work is for, what problem it solves, what proof matters, and what action the right visitor should take. The design and code then carry that structure cleanly across desktop and mobile.

Build Standard

Strategic copy, responsive code, accessible structure, version control, and a deployment the owner can actually keep.
